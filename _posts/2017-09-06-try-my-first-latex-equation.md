The *Gamma function* satisfying $$\Gamma(n) = (n-1)!\quad\forall
n\in\mathbb N$$ is via through the Euler integral

$$
\Gamma(z) = \int_0^\infty t^{z-1}e^{-t}dt\,.
$$
